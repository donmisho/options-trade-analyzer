## Relevant Context — Do Not Deviate Without Escalation

**Source: insight_engine.md §3.6 (adjustment phase)**
An **adjustment** runs after the raw score exists; it can read the score and the inputs, and adds/subtracts a configured amount or forces a floor/cap. Per the pipeline, adjustments clamp to `[0,100]` after each.

**Source: migration plan (S4.3) — cushion penalty (OTA-728)**
Move `_cushion_penalty` from `strategy_scorer.py:138-142` into a registered post-scoring-adjustment formula. The graduated penalty is **decomposed into two atomic adjustment rules** (atoms seeded under OTA-680). Per-strategy thresholds — the former 1.0% / 2.0% literals — come from junction rows, so SP and WG can carry different values.

**Source: migration plan (S4.4) + audit §7.2 — asymmetry penalty (OTA-729)**
Move `scoring_factors/asymmetry.py` into a registered post-scoring-adjustment formula. The thresholds (2.0, 1.5, 1.25) and penalties (25, 15, 8) come from junction rows, **not** literals in the formula.

**Source: CLAUDE.md / insight_engine.md — purity + no literals**
Pure `(named_values, params) -> float in [0,100]` (or a delta the engine applies, per the adjustment contract); thresholds/penalties via `params`; no shared state.

**Source: OTA-689 / OTA-699** — registered names must appear in the SHARED `formula_registry` set; OTA-699 validates membership/drift at load.
